'''Star Thief''' ('''Ditmal Pirval''') is a [[fiction]]al [[Marvel Comics]] [[character (arts)|character]].  

==Fictional character biography==
While out driving with his wife and two kids outside New Delhi, Ditmal Pirval was struck by a bolt of energy from a malfunctioning satellite. This bolt kills his family and gives him extensive energy based [[Superpower (ability)|powers]].  This motivates him to adopt the costumed identity of Star Thief and attempt to end all [[human]] exploration of space <ref> New Warriors #5 </ref>.  He was dissuaded from this goal after a violent confrontation with the [[New Warriors]] and the [[Inhumans]]. In a more thoughtful mood, he lived with and learned from the Inhumans for a time,{{issue|date=May 2011}} but has not been seen during the Inhumans' last few years of activity.
