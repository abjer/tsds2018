'''Al MacKenzie''' is a [[fictional character]] appearing in [[American comic book]]s published by [[Marvel Comics]].

==Publication history==
Al MacKenzie first appeared in ''[[Nick Fury vs. S.H.I.E.L.D.]]'' #3 (August 1988), and was created by [[Bob Harras]] and [[Paul Neary]].

The character subsequently appears in ''[[Nick Fury, Agent of S.H.I.E.L.D. (comic book)|Nick Fury, Agent of S.H.I.E.L.D.]]'' #1-7 (September 1989-January 1990).

Al MacKenzie received an entry in the ''[[Official Handbook of the Marvel Universe|Official Handbook of the Marvel Universe Update '89]]'' #4.

==Fictional character biography==
Al MacKenzie was born in [[Austin, Texas]]. He was once the [[Central Intelligence Agency|C.I.A.]] liaison to [[S.H.I.E.L.D.]]<ref>''Official Handbook of the Marvel Universe'' Vol 3 #4</ref> He became romantically involved with Contessa [[Valentina Allegra di Fontaine]] after a while, which led to an estrangement between him and [[Nick Fury]].<ref>''Nick Fury, Agent of S.H.I.E.L.D.'' Vol. 3 #1</ref> Because of this, he returned to the CIA with the Contessa.<ref>''Nick Fury, Agent of S.H.I.E.L.D.'' Vol. 3 #3</ref>

MacKenzie later joined the S.H.I.E.L.D. organization full-time, and spent considerable time as the senior liaison officer to the C.I.A.<ref>''Nick Fury, Agent of S.H.I.E.L.D.'' Vol. 3 #6</ref> Subsequently, under unknown circumstances, MacKenzie resigned from S.H.I.E.L.D., and wrote a "tell-all" book entitled ''UnSHIELDed: an Unauthorized Insider's Look Behind the World's Most Powerful Global Spy Network'', which purportedly explained some of the history behind the organization from his point of view.<ref>''[[Amazing Fantasy]]'' Vol. 2, # 10</ref> Since then, he also acted as an unofficial source to [[Ben Urich]], prodding Urich and [[Jessica Jones]] to attempt to expose Fury's unauthorized mission to [[Latveria]].<ref>''[[The Pulse (comics)|The Pulse]]'' # 9</ref>
